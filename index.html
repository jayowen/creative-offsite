Brainstorm Guide

Use these categories when planning each series element. Each prompt helps ensure comprehensive creative planning.

1

Title

What are some title options? What title would help people remember the content?

2

Look/Feel

What does staging and environment look like at each campus?

3

Branding/Design

What are some ideas for branding? Gather photos or links to design inspiration.

4

Story/Video

What would the video elements look like? What life change stories could we tell?

5

Response

Are there any weeks we could call people to a specific action during response time?

6

In-Service Elements

In addition to singing, what are ways we can engage people in worship prior to preaching?

7

Connect/Next Steps

What events or next step opportunities? Resources to provide? Ministry partnerships?

8

Kingdom Resources

Content we could create? Social media opportunities? Resources to give people?

9

Music

Specific song ideas for this series? Any new songs we don't currently sing?

10

Generosity

Are there stories of generosity we can tell this series?
