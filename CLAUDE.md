# Creative Offsite - The Church of Eleven22

## Overview
Planning materials for The Church of Eleven22's creative team offsite (February 3-4, 2025). The team plans sermon series content, worship elements, special events, and ministry programming.

## Organization
- **Church:** The Church of Eleven22 (multi-campus evangelical church)
- **Teaching Framework:** "Matthew Seasons" - systematic teaching through the Gospel of Matthew
- **Lead Pastor:** Pastor Joby
- **Key Staff:** Pastor Britt, Bo, Ali

## Documents

### FEB 2026 Offsite Schedule
Two-day agenda for the creative offsite at The Retreat Center covering:
- Bible study and brainstorming sessions
- Series planning (Neighbors to Nations, Saturated)
- Special events (Neighborhood Nights, Commitment Night)
- Special services planning (Easter, prison service, New Year's Eve)

### Creative Offsite Pre-Worksheet
Brainstorming template with 10 planning categories:
1. **Title** - Series naming options
2. **Look/Feel** - Staging and environment
3. **Branding/Design** - Visual identity and design inspiration
4. **Story/Video** - Video elements and life change stories
5. **Response** - Call-to-action moments during services
6. **In-Service Elements** - Worship engagement beyond singing
7. **Connect/Next Steps** - Events, resources, ministry partnerships
8. **Kingdom Resources** - Social media, downloadable content
9. **Music** - Song selection and new music
10. **Generosity** - Giving stories and additional creative ideas
